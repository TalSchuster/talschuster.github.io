Biography

I’m a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL and a member of the NLP and Learn To Cure groups. My advisor is Prof. Regina Barzilay.
Currently, I’m focusing on multi-lingual transfer for zero-shot learning and other low resource scenarios. I have also worked on creating Deep-Learning screening based models to improve cancer detection and risk assessment.
Before coming to MIT, I was an MSc student in Computer Science at Tel Aviv University, advised by Prof. Lior Wolf.
