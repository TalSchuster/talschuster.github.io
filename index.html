Biography

I’m a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL and a member of the NLP and Learn To Cure groups. My advisor is Prof. Regina Barzilay.
Currently, I’m focusing on automatic fact-checking and on the geometry of contextual word embeddings. I have also worked on creating deep learning screening-based methods to improve cancer detection and risk assessment.
Before coming to MIT, I was an MSc student in Computer Science at Tel Aviv University, advised by Prof. Lior Wolf.
